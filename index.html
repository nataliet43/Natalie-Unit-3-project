Street Cat

It was just a regular day walking from the store you weren't paying attention to your surroundings until you bumped into something on the floor you look down and see a cat in a box you think about it and if you should leave it there or take it home with you

You think about it and decide to bring the cat home with you so you pick up the box and start walking home

You look down at it again and decide it would probably be best if you left it there since you don't know why it was left there

You get home and clean up the cat and give it some food then after some thinking you know you made the right decision since it was a really cute cat and now you have a new pet

When you get home and sit down you really thought about the cat and how it could be abandoned in the cold weather you start to feel bad for it now and realise you made a mistake not taking the cat
